About Me

Hi, I'm Natalie,

UX/UI designer who creates meaningful experiences where usability comes first. I don't just make things look good - I make sure they make sense.

I believe great products come from collaboration. I enjoy engaging in team discussions, aligning with business goals, and respecting technical constraints, while also confidently making independent, data-driven decisions with the user experience in mind.

I find my flow and inspiration outdoors and in motion. Whether it’s snowboarding down a mountain, hiking, kayaking, or exploring caves through caving and rock climbing, I’m always up for an adventure. From cycling to pole dancing - the list of my hobbies is endless, but they all serve one purpose: keeping my mind sharp and my energy high for the next big challenge.
